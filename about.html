About Us!

Festivals is a digital hub that celebrates cultural diversity through the lens of global festivals. Our platform brings together stories, traditions, and visuals from celebrations around the world — from the lights of Diwali to the spirit of Lunar New Year. With a modern and inclusive approach, Festivals is your gateway to discovering the beauty of our shared humanity through festive moments.
