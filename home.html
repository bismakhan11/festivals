Islamic Festival: Eid-ul-Fitr

Indian Festival: Diwali

Diwali, the Festival of Lights, celebrates the victory of light over darkness and good over evil. Homes glow with lamps, fireworks fill the sky, and families come together to share sweets, prayers, and joy! learn more about it click the button below.

Holi Festival

Eid-ul-Adha: Islamic Festival

Eid-ul-Adha, the Festival of Sacrifice, honors the devotion of Prophet Ibrahim and his willingness to obey God's command. It is marked by prayer, charity, and the sharing of meat, symbolizing faith, compassion, and community. to lean more click the button below.

Lunar New Year

Lunar New Year, also known as Chinese New Year or Spring Festival, marks the beginning of the lunar calendar. Celebrated with family gatherings, feasts, red decorations, and fireworks, it symbolizes renewal, luck, and prosperity. to lean more click the button below.
